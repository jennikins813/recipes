Ingredients:
    - 1 Tbsp instant coffee
    - hot water
    - sugar, to taste
    - half and half, or milk, or other coffee creamer of choice

Directions:
    1. In your favorite mug, add instant coffee granules.
    2. Pour hot water into mug to desired level, and stir. Be careful not to burn yourself!
    3. Add sugar to taste, and coffee creamer of your choice.
    4. Sip and enjoy!
